Privacy Policy

2048 — Mobile Game  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2025

🔒

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how the 2048 app handles your information. The short version: we don't collect, store, or share any personal data. This game is built purely for fun.

01

Information We Collect

The 2048 app does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal details.

No account creation or login is required to play the game.

✅  No personal data is collected — ever.
02

Automatically Collected Data

The app may store minimal data locally on your device only (such as your high score and game progress). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

  • High score & game state — stored locally on device
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are embedded in the app
03

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or crash reporting at this time. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly.

The app is distributed via Google Play Store, which has its own privacy policy. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on data collected through the Play Store platform itself.

04

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose to any third party. We do not share any information with advertisers, partners, or any external entity.

05

Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because we do not collect any personal information, the app complies with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

06

Permissions

The app requests only the minimum permissions necessary for it to function:

  • Storage — to save your game progress locally on the device
  • No camera, microphone, location, or contacts permission is requested
07

Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the risk to your personal information is minimal. Game data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

You can always find the latest version of this policy at the GitHub repository: github.com/mohit-52/game_2048
